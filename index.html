Case Studies

Issue #01 · Platform Build

Wealthtech Platform — Zero to Scale

A full-stack MFD platform built from scratch: client portal, ops engine, 9 live API integrations, and a full compliance layer.

₹400Cr
AUM Powered

The Challenge

No technology infrastructure existed. The business needed a client app, an internal ops portal, regulatory KYC, and live market integrations — all on a startup budget and tight timeline.

The Approach

A modular architecture separating client, ops, and data layers. Integrations prioritized by regulatory necessity (DigiLocker, KFin, CAMS), then market data (NSE/BSE), then growth tooling. Shipped in phases.

Delivered

Client portal with live NAV, P&L tracking, and document vault. Ops portal with KYC automation and compliance dashboards. A 9-partner API layer end-to-end.

₹400Cr+ AUM 1,400+ Clients 9 Integrations 80% Faster Onboarding 75% Faster Transactions
Issue #02 · Growth Campaign

Muhurat Campaign — 48-Hour Sprint

Conceived, designed, and executed a time-bound investment campaign generating ₹1.5–2 Cr in inflows within a single Diwali trading window.

48hr
₹2Cr Inflows

The Opportunity

Muhurat Trading — Diwali's auspicious session — creates a unique window of high investor intent. The challenge: mobilize clients rapidly while the platform held under surge load.

The Execution

End-to-end campaign design: personalized outreach, product-selection rationale, pre-cleared compliance, platform load testing, and real-time transaction monitoring — tech, ops, and clients in parallel.

Impact

High-velocity campaigns demand platform resilience, pre-staged compliance, and strong HNI trust. The sprint validated platform capacity under concentrated load.

₹1.5–2Cr Inflows 48-Hour Window Zero Downtime Multi-Client Coordination
Issue #03 · Agentic Automation

Legacy Business — Automated End-to-End with AI Agents

Rebuilt a legacy operation from manual to autonomous: agentic n8n workflows now run client interactions, portfolio reviews, document storage, CRM, and task management with minimal human lift.

100%
Hands-Off Ops

The Challenge

A legacy business ran on manual, disconnected processes — client follow-ups, portfolio reviews, paperwork, and task tracking all done by hand. Slow, repetitive, and easy to let slip.

The Approach

Designed and built agentic workflows in n8n that chain AI steps with the platform's integrations — mapping each manual process into an event-driven pipeline, with humans-in-the-loop only where judgement is needed.

Impact

Client interactions, portfolio reviews, document storage, CRM operations, and task management now run automatically — freeing the team to focus on advice and relationships instead of admin.
