Wealthtech Platform — Zero to Scale
Full-stack MFD platform built from scratch: client portal, ops engine, 9 live API integrations, and full compliance layer.
Read Case Study
The Challenge

No technology infrastructure existed at all. The business simultaneously needed a client-facing app, an internal ops portal, regulatory KYC capability, and live market integrations — all under a startup budget and tight timeline.

The Approach

Designed a modular architecture separating client, ops, and data layers. Prioritized integrations by regulatory necessity first (DigiLocker, KFin, CAMS), then market data (NSE/BSE), then growth tooling. Shipped in phases.

What Was Delivered

Client portal with live NAV, portfolio P&L tracking, transaction history, and document vault. MFD ops portal with KYC automation, order management, and compliance dashboards. Full API layer across 9 partners: DigiLocker, NSE/BSE, KFin, CAMS, MFU, LIC/ICICI, Star Health, and payment gateways.

Muhurat Campaign — 48hr ₹2Cr Sprint
Conceived, designed, and executed a time-bound investment campaign generating ₹1.5–2Cr in inflows within a single Diwali trading window.
Read Case Study
The Opportunity

Muhurat Trading — Diwali's auspicious session — creates a unique window of high investor intent and emotional readiness. The challenge was mobilizing clients rapidly while ensuring the platform held under surge load.

The Execution

End-to-end campaign design: personalized client outreach, product selection rationale, pre-cleared compliance checks, platform load testing, and real-time transaction monitoring. Tech, ops, and client management running in parallel.

Learnings & Impact

High-velocity campaigns require platform resilience, pre-staged compliance, and strong HNI relationships. The campaign validated the platform's capacity under pressure and confirmed that concentrated inflows are possible when trust and tech are both in place.
